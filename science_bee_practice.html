🔒 Privacy & Data Notice
  • ✔ Voice audio never leaves your device. Speech recognition now uses a local AI model (OpenAI Whisper tiny) that runs entirely inside your browser via WebAssembly. Your microphone audio is processed on-device only — it is never uploaded to any server.
  • ✔ One-time model download (~40 MB). The first time you use voice mode, the browser downloads the Whisper model from Hugging Face. After that it is cached by your browser — no further downloads occur and the app works fully offline.
  • ✔ No-Mic mode is also fully private. No audio, no network, nothing leaves the device. Grade answers manually with the ✓/✗ buttons.
